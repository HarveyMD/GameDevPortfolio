About Me
I am a recent graduate from the University of Derby with a bachelor's degree in Computer Games Programming.

My main specialties include game mechanics and feature programming, UI programming in collaboration with artists to build aesthetically pleasing and fluid UI systems, build testing and debugging, and level design/greymapping.

I primarily work with Unreal Engine and Unity, with a strong preference for Unreal Engine due to its versatility and advanced tools.

I actively seek opportunities to gain experience through game jams and engage in personal projects to stay sharp and continuously improve my coding skills.

I am passionate about enhancing my abilities in game development, team communication, and problem-solving while contributing to innovative and engaging gaming experiences.

Technical Skills
