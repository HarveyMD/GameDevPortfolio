Fallen Hero Game Jam
This was a 10-day game jam project developed as part of the Game Academy Bootcamp. I took on the role of team leader and programmer, helping guide the overall development process and keeping things on track for the deadline.

We built the game in Unity, and I focused mainly on level design, creating the character movement system, implementing obstacle interactions, and setting up a basic NPC dialogue system. Despite the time pressure, we delivered a fun and playable experience, and I learned a lot about rapid iteration, teamwork, and balancing scope with creativity.
