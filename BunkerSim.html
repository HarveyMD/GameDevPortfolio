3D Bunker Simulation

This Unity-based project was developed in a week as part of a game development challenge from Antimatter Games, in collaboration with Derby University.

A team of six—three programmers and three artists—worked together to create a detailed and immersive Cold War-era bunker scene, designed to capture the tension and atmosphere of the time period.

This project demonstrated our ability to work efficiently under tight deadlines while maintaining high standards of quality and realism.

My Contributions:

Basic Main Menu – Designed and implemented a simple main menu for the demo, allowing players to navigate the game efficiently.

Greyboxing & Level Layout – Designed the grayscale map of the bunker, carefully planning its intricate layout to establish structure, flow, and realism.

Interior Design & Scene Composition – Created and arranged three distinct rooms, each reflecting different aspects of the Cold War-era setting, enhancing environmental storytelling and immersion.

Environmental Detail – Contributed to the overall atmosphere of the bunker by refining lighting, object placement, and scene aesthetics to ensure authenticity.
