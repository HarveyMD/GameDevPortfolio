Graphics 1
This was a solo project I worked on during my second year at university, where I built a simple 3D graphics engine from scratch in C++. I used custom vector and matrix math to handle all the transformations and rendering

It started off as a 2D rotating square, and over time I expanded it into a 3D cube that could rotate around the Y-axis. Eventually, I added a basic model loader so I could import pre-made 3D models and apply transformations like rotation and translation. I also wrote a scaling function, but I ended up leaving that out of the final version I presented.

This project really helped me get a solid grasp of how 3D graphics work under the hood and gave me a much deeper appreciation for linear algebra and low-level rendering.
